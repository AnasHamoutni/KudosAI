ABOUT ME

My name is Anas HAMOUTNI and I am a statistician/programmer, which translates to data analyst/scientist in modern vocabulary. I have an engineering degree from the National Institute of Statistics and Applied Economics, (INSEA), in Rabat, Morocco.

Since I was young, I have always enjoyed solving puzzles and this is exactly how I approach every data problem that I encounter. For me, finding new patterns and designing creative solutions has always been the main motivation.

I have more than 2 years of experience, mainly in the insurance industry, during which I was able to experiment with many ideas and produce ready-to-use, innovative, solutions. These experiences have allowed me to develop my analytical and problem-solving skills, my social abilities, and my teamwork capacity.
